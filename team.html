About This Project

This Advanced Statistics Linear Regression Calculator was created collaboratively to simplify statistical computations. Our tool allows users to calculate regression equations, correlation coefficients, visualize scatter plots, and perform hypothesis testing with intuitive controls.

We aimed to combine accuracy, interactivity, and modern design to make learning statistics engaging and effective.
