About This Project

This Advanced Statistics Linear Regression Calculator was developed as a collaborative effort to provide an easy-to-use tool for statistical analysis. The application allows users to input data points and automatically calculates regression equations, correlation coefficients, and visualizes the data through scatter diagrams.

Our team worked together to combine mathematical precision with user-friendly design, ensuring that statistical analysis is accessible to everyone.
